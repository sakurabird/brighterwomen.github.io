【Details】
今回はレスポンシブなWebサイトの構築方法を学び、更にシリコンバレーでのWeb制作、 モバイルアプリケーション制作のトレンドをご紹介いたします。

Intro to Creating Websites

【Speaker】Justin Clune

Justin Clune is a California native who came to Japan 3 years ago to get a masters in Engineering at the University of Tokyo.
He has experience as a website developer at an internet startup and at CyberAgent.
He recently began a startup with the vision to help working parents continue their career dreams.

【Details】

Learn how to make a responsive website! This is a casual introduction to using Twitter Bootstrap, HTML, and CSS.
There will be live coding so please bring your laptop to create a simple self bio website. Also learn some important trends in websites and mobile apps.

[About the event]
The goal is to encourage women to gain the skills to do programming or design in Internet companies.
Most people who participate have beginner level programming experience or no experience.
Please bring your own laptop. It will help you greatly to prepare for the event by playing with HTML and CSS here: http://www.w3schools.com/ Bonus points for playing with javascript!
